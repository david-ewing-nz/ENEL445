ENEL445 — Applied Engineering Optimisation

David Ewing  |  University of Canterbury  |  2026

Gradient-based optimisation for variational inference: CAVI baseline, Gibbs MCMC gold standard, and course methods (gradient ascent, Newton, BFGS) applied to maximise the ELBO.

  • Combined Report

    All three case studies — linear, quadratic, and logistic Bayesian regression. Cross-case comparison and discussion.

  • Case Study 1 — Linear Regression

    Full ELBO derivation, gradient verification, ELBO landscape, convergence figures, posterior comparison, and variance collapse analysis. n=50, p=2.

  • Case Study 2 — Quadratic Regression

    Identical framework with p=3 and quadratic feature expansion. n=100.

  • Case Study 3 — Logistic Regression

    Jaakkola–Jordan variational bound, Pólya–Gamma Gibbs sampler, ESS diagnostics, and 20 result figures. n=200, p=2.

  • Case Study 4 — Hierarchical Bayesian Linear Regression

    Mixed-effects model with J=5 groups, CAVI and BFGS vs Newton-Raphson and GA. Severe posterior variance collapse for β0 (SD ratio 0.130). N=150, p=2, D=19.

  • Case Study 5 — Hierarchical Bayesian Logistic Regression

    Jaakkola–Jordan ELBO with random intercepts, Pólya–Gamma Gibbs gold standard. CAVI 1050× faster than Gibbs; symmetric variance collapse (β0 and β1 SD ratio ~0.46). N=150, p=2, D=17.
