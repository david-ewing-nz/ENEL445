## Page 2

Classification: In-Confidence
New course 
(2024 – special topic, 2025 onwards – permanent course)
• Why optimisation:
• Optimisation is used a lot in industry and is one of the 
things industry have told us to teach more about.
• Many hot topics (e.g. Artificial Intelligence / Machine 
Learning, Smart Grids, etc.) are based on optimisation. 
• If any of you are thinking about postgrad, the techniques 
taught in this course are likely to be useful to you.
• This is still a new course – please be aware that we 
don’t have everything sorted and let us know if you 
find any issues.

to calculate the final grade.

Tests or Exam.
