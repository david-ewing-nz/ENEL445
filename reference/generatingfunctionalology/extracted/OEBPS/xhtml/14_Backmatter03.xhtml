References

An excellent general reference on generating functions is [Co], which contains a wealth of beautiful examples. The volume [GJ] is highly recommended to those who wish to study deeper and more varied uses of generating functions. For excellent surveys of combinatorial asymptotics, see [Be], and [Od]. For other methods that can cope with a wide variety of combinatorial identities see [Eg],[Kn], vol. 1, and [GKP]. For assortments of unapologetically difficult problems in asymptotics with advanced solution techniques, see [Br] and [GK]. [Go] is a catalogue of binomial coefficient identities.
